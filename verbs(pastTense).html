Verbs: Past-tense

In chapter 3, we learned about the present-tense conjugation of verbs. In this chapter, we will learn about the past-tense conjugation of verbs. Here are the past-tense conjugations for each type of verb and each pronoun:

(cantar, comer, and abrir are used as examples)
Yo

AR verbs: -é

ER verbs: -í

IR verbs: -í

Example

Yo canté (I sang)

Yo comí (I ate)

Yo abrí (I opened)

Tu

AR verbs: -aste

ER verbs: -iste

IR verbs: -iste

Example

Tu cantaste (You sang)

Tu comiste (You ate)

Tu abriste (You opened)

El/Ella/Usted

AR verbs: -ó

ER verbs: -ió

IR verbs: -ió

Example

El cantó (He sang)

Ella comió (she ate)

Usted abrió (You opened)

Nosotros

AR verbs: -amos

ER verbs: -emos

IR verbs: -imos

Example

Nosotros cantamos (We sang)

Nosotros comimos (We ate)

Nosotros abrimos (We opened)

Vosotros

 AR verbs: -asteis

ER verbs: -isteis

IR verbs: -isteis

Example

Vosotros cantasteis
(You all sang)

Vosotros comisteis
(You all ate)

Vosotros abristeis
(You all opened)

Ellos/Ellas/
Ustedes

AR verbs: -aron

ER verbs: -ieron

IR verbs: -ieron

Example

Ellos cantaron (They sang)

Ellos comieron (They ate)

Ellos abrieron (They opened)
